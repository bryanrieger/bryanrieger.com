    Thank you for stopping by.

    Hello! I’m Bryan Rieger, an artist, designer, and worrier writer focused on the absurd, the fantastic, and the utterly mundane aspects of life. My work tends to explore the unexpected and the everyday with humour and joy.

    Over the years I’ve worked in creative fields such as software design, illustration, animation, theatre, and publishing. Today, I’m taking everything I’ve learned along the way and applying it to my own body of creative work.
